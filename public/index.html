I work at the intersection of Software Defined Networking and network protocol research — asking how we can decouple the intelligence of a network from its physical substrate to make infrastructure adaptive, verifiable, and observable.

Outside research, I build production-grade systems with the MERN stack and bring a competitive programmer's obsession with correctness and performance to every line of C++ I write.
